# Praxant Fresh Logo Set

This directory now contains a new logo exploration authored from scratch for Praxant.

These concepts were built to avoid the prior motif family used elsewhere in the repo:

- no ant imagery
- no node-network diagrams
- no hexagons
- no orbital layouts
- no anvils
- no bracket/container marks
- no single-letter `P` monograms

## Concepts

### 01. Control Dial
Why: Praxant is fundamentally about control over autonomy. The dial suggests deliberate control, tunable authority, and operator-adjusted power.
Strong points:
- clear control metaphor
- works well as product infrastructure branding
- scales cleanly
Weak points:
- can feel slightly operational rather than expressive
- dial metaphors are common in systems software

### 02. Signal Weave
Why: Different runtimes and providers are woven into one supervised workflow. The mark emphasizes orchestration rather than raw execution.
Strong points:
- feels dynamic and layered
- suggests composition without a literal network
- visually distinctive in motion
Weak points:
- abstraction may be less immediate
- can read more like integration middleware than review workflow

### 03. Policy Gate
Why: Teams use Praxant to decide what agents are allowed to do. The mark centers on approval, thresholds, and governed execution.
Strong points:
- expresses policy and review clearly
- stable, serious, enterprise-friendly
- easy to pair with trust-and-control messaging
Weak points:
- more conservative than the bolder concepts
- less expressive of runtime neutrality

### 04. Runtime Ribbon
Why: Multiple agent runtimes flow into one orchestration layer. The converging ribbons emphasize provider neutrality and unified workflow.
Strong points:
- strong story for multi-runtime positioning
- fluid and modern without feeling trendy
- good for animation
Weak points:
- can feel abstract without explanation
- less obviously about local control

### 05. Local Beacon
Why: Agents run on your machine under your control. The beacon suggests a local operating center rather than a hosted black box.
Strong points:
- clear local-first metaphor
- memorable silhouette
- good fit for self-hosted positioning
Weak points:
- can read slightly like networking or observability tooling
- less explicit about orchestration

### 06. Relay Stack
Why: Praxant supervises work moving through layered systems: runtime, policy, review, and merge. The stack communicates structured progression.
Strong points:
- strong infrastructure feel
- good fit for technical documentation and product UI
- scalable shape language
Weak points:
- less emotionally distinctive
- could be mistaken for generic platform infrastructure

### 07. Sovereign Window
Why: The product promise is visibility and control over agent behavior. This concept frames execution as something visible, bounded, and inspectable.
Strong points:
- communicates transparency and control
- feels calm and serious
- visually simple
Weak points:
- less dynamic than the more fluid concepts
- may need stronger messaging support to feel unique

### 08. Merge Fork
Why: Many possible agent paths are resolved into one reviewed path forward. This fits Praxant's orchestration and decision workflow.
Strong points:
- suggests guidance and convergence
- strong directional energy
- visually compact
Weak points:
- can read as version-control specific
- narrower product story than some alternatives

### 09. Review Lens
Why: Praxant is not blind automation. The lens suggests human-readable review, inspectability, and code-understanding before progression.
Strong points:
- strongest review-and-visibility metaphor
- approachable without being playful
- distinctive in documentation and product surfaces
Weak points:
- may understate automation
- lens metaphors can skew analytical rather than operational

### 10. Command Knot
Why: The orchestration layer ties runtimes, policy, and workflow into one controlled system. The knot suggests coordination under tension without chaos.
Strong points:
- unique abstract identity
- feels durable and ownable
- broad enough for long-term brand use
Weak points:
- most abstract concept in the set
- needs explanation before the meaning is obvious

## Recommendation

The strongest fresh directions are:

- `fresh-01-control-dial.svg` for the clearest control-centric positioning
- `fresh-04-runtime-ribbon.svg` for multi-runtime orchestration
- `fresh-10-command-knot.svg` for a more ownable long-term identity

Open `preview.html` to review all ten on light and dark surfaces.
